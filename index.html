How to Make Pancakes

Follow these simple steps to create fluffy, delicious pancakes!

Step 1: Gather Ingredients

You will need flour, milk, eggs, sugar, baking powder, and a pinch of salt.

Step 2: Mix Dry Ingredients

Combine flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt in a large mixing bowl.

Step 3: Add Wet Ingredients

Add milk and eggs to the dry mixture. Stir until smooth.

Step 4: Heat Pan

Heat a non-stick pan over medium heat and lightly grease with butter or oil.

Step 5: Cook Pancakes

Pour batter onto the pan and cook until bubbles form. Flip and cook the other side.

Step 6: Serve and Enjoy

Stack the pancakes, drizzle with syrup, and add your favorite toppings. Enjoy!
